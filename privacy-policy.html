4. Data Storage

All data created by Call2Pray is stored locally on your device only:

  • User preferences: Settings like Adhan selection, calculation method, dark mode
  • Last known location: Cached to reduce GPS battery usage
  • Prayer time cache: Pre-calculated times to work offline

This data is stored in your device's local storage using Android's SharedPreferences. It is:

  • Never transmitted over the internet
  • Never shared with any third party
  • Never backed up to cloud services
  • Automatically deleted when you uninstall the app

5. Internet Connectivity

Call2Pray does NOT require internet to function. However, optional internet access may be used for:

  • Reverse geocoding: Converting GPS coordinates to city/country names (first time only)
  • That's it. No analytics, no tracking, no ads, no data collection.

After the initial location lookup, the app works completely offline.

6. Third-Party Services

Call2Pray does NOT integrate with or use any third-party services including:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud storage services

7. Children's Privacy

Call2Pray does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is designed to be safe for all ages and contains no inappropriate content, data collection, or external links.

8. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there's nothing for us to:
